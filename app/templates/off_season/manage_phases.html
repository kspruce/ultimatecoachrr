Phase Planning Guidelines
Effective Phase Structure
  • Each phase should have a clear, distinct focus
  • Phases should build progressively on each other
  • Consider appropriate duration (typically 4-6 weeks)
  • Ensure smooth transitions between phases
Content Development
  • Balance volume and intensity appropriately
  • Include specific, measurable outcomes
  • Consider recovery needs within the phase
  • Align with seasonal periodization principles
