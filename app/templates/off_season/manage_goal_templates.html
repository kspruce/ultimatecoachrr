SMART Goal Guidelines

When creating goal templates, follow the SMART criteria:

  • Specific: Clear, well-defined goals that players can understand
  • Measurable: Include metrics to track progress
  • Achievable: Challenging but attainable within the phase timeframe
  • Relevant: Aligned with the phase focus and ultimate frisbee skills
  • Time-bound: Set within the phase timeframe
