    Overview

    A comprehensive 16-week progressive track workout plan designed to build speed, power, and endurance for ultimate frisbee players.

    Week 0: Intervals - Foundation
    4 × (2min on, 2min active recovery)

    Focus: Building aerobic base and teaching your body to work harder than it thinks it can.

    Week 1: Intervals - Consistency
    4 × (2min on, 2min active recovery)

    Focus: Establish consistency before increasing intensity.

    Week 2: Intervals - Volume
    6 × (90s on, 90s active recovery)

    Focus: Increased volume with shorter, more intense efforts.

    Week 3: Intervals - Intensity
    6 × (90s on, 90s active recovery)

    Focus: Continue building aerobic base and mental toughness.

    Week 4: Intervals - Peak
    6 × (90s on, 90s active recovery)

    Focus: Final week of interval base - prepare for next phase.

    Week 5: Walk-Jog-Run - Introduction
    7 × (walk 50m, jog 200m, run 100m)

    Focus: Transition to faster running with technique focus.

    Week 6: Walk-Jog-Run - Volume
    8 × (walk 50m, jog 200m, run 100m)

    Focus: Increased volume while maintaining speed.

    Week 7: Walk-Jog-Run - Peak
    9 × (walk 50m, jog 200m, run 100m)

    Focus: Maximum volume for this phase.

    Week 8: Hills - Introduction
    Hill Sprints

    Focus: 7 runs up hill, 20 seconds each. Walk down recovery.

    Week 9: Hills - Power
    Hill Sprints

    Focus: Maintain power and speed with good technique.

    Week 10: Hills - Progression
    Hill Sprints

    Focus: Reach higher points with explosive drive.

    Week 11: Hills - Peak
    Hill Sprints

    Focus: Maximum effort for hill phase completion.

    Week 12: Sprints - Acceleration
    2 sets of 8 accelerations (20m)

    Focus: Using power to get up to speed quickly from low start position.

    Week 13: Sprints - Lead-Ins
    4 sets of 3 lead-ins (40m: 20m build-up, 20m sprint)

    Focus: Build-up to full speed with technique focus.

    Week 14: Sprints - Pyramid
    30-80m pyramid (30,40,50,60,70,80,70,60,50,40,30m)

    Focus: Full sprint mechanics at various distances.

    Week 15: Sprints - Full Speed
    7 × 100m sprints

    Focus: Maximum speed with good form.

    Training Guidelines
    Weekly Structure
    • Track Work: 1-2 sessions per week
    • Recovery: 48-72 hours between sessions
    • Complementary: Continue strength training
    Progression
    • Listen to your body
    • Focus on form first
    • Allow adequate recovery
