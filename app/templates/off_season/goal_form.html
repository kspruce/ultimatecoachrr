    Title: Improve Vertical Jump

    Category: Fitness

    Target Date: 3 months from now

    • Specific: Increase my vertical jump height by 4 inches.
    • Measurable: I will measure my progress weekly using the vertical jump test against a wall.
    • Achievable: I will follow a specific plyometric training program 3 times per week and maintain proper nutrition.
    • Relevant: Improving my vertical jump will help me contest more discs in the air during games.
    • Time-bound: I will achieve this goal within 12 weeks by following a progressive training schedule.

    Title: Master Forehand Huck

    Category: Skills

    Target Date: 2 months from now

    • Specific: Develop a consistent forehand huck that can accurately reach targets 40+ yards downfield.
    • Measurable: I will track my completion percentage during practice, aiming for 70% accuracy at 40+ yards.
    • Achievable: I will practice forehand throws for 20 minutes daily, focusing on form and distance control.
    • Relevant: Adding a reliable forehand huck to my throwing arsenal will make me a more versatile offensive threat.
    • Time-bound: I will achieve this goal within 8 weeks by following a structured throwing progression.

    Title: Develop Pre-Game Mental Routine

    Category: Mental

    Target Date: 1 month from now

    • Specific: Create and consistently implement a 15-minute pre-game mental preparation routine.
    • Measurable: I will track my adherence to the routine before each practice and game, and rate my mental focus on a scale of 1-10.
    • Achievable: I will research effective mental preparation techniques and develop a personalized routine that includes visualization, breathing exercises, and positive affirmations.
    • Relevant: A consistent mental preparation routine will help me perform more consistently under pressure.
    • Time-bound: I will develop the routine within 2 weeks and then practice it consistently for the following 2 weeks before every training session and game.
