    About the Track Workout Plan

    The track workout plan is a 12-week program designed to improve speed, agility, and conditioning for ultimate frisbee players during the off-season.

    Each week can be linked to a session plan from your session library, allowing players to access detailed workout instructions.

    Tips for Creating Effective Workouts
    • Ensure progressive overload throughout the 12 weeks
    • Include a mix of linear speed, lateral movement, and change of direction drills
    • Incorporate sport-specific conditioning elements
    • Provide clear instructions and technique cues in session plans
    • Consider creating beginner, intermediate, and advanced variations
