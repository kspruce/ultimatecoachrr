Workout Tips
  • Always perform a proper warm-up before beginning the workout.
  • Focus on proper form and technique over weight or speed.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the workout.
  • If you're new to an exercise, start with lighter weights or modifications.
  • Cool down and stretch after completing the workout.
Important Notes
Health & Safety

Consult with a healthcare professional before beginning any new exercise program, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions.

Modifications

Adjust the workout intensity as needed based on your current fitness level. It's better to complete the workout with proper form at a lower intensity than to risk injury.

Progression

As you get stronger, gradually increase the challenge by adding weight, reps, or reducing rest periods.
