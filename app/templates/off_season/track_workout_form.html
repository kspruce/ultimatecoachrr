    Tips for Creating Effective Track Workouts
    Speed Development
    • Include acceleration drills (10-30m sprints)
    • Add maximum velocity work (flying 20s)
    • Incorporate resisted and assisted sprints
    • Focus on proper sprint mechanics
    Agility Training
    • Use cone drills for change of direction
    • Practice lateral movement patterns
    • Include reactive agility drills
    • Work on deceleration and reacceleration
    Conditioning
    • Implement interval training (work:rest ratios)
    • Use shuttle runs and tempo runs
    • Add sport-specific conditioning drills
    • Progress volume and intensity gradually
    Recovery
    • Include proper warm-up and cool-down protocols
    • Balance high-intensity days with recovery days
    • Consider active recovery options
    • Provide mobility work recommendations
