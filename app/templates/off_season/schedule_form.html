    Standard Schedule Information

    The standard schedule is designed for athletes who can commit to a regular training routine with balanced volume and intensity.

    • Typical Sessions: 3-4 per week
    • Session Duration: 45-75 minutes
    • Intensity: Moderate to high
    • Ideal For: Athletes with some training experience and moderate time availability
    Minimal Schedule Information

    The minimal schedule is designed for athletes with limited time, focusing on the most essential training elements to maintain progress.

    • Typical Sessions: 2-3 per week
    • Session Duration: 30-45 minutes
    • Intensity: Moderate
    • Ideal For: Beginners, athletes with limited time, or those returning from injury
    High Volume Schedule Information

    The high volume schedule is designed for athletes who can commit to additional training sessions and want to maximize their development during this phase.

    • Typical Sessions: 5-6 per week
    • Session Duration: 60-90 minutes
    • Intensity: Moderate to very high
    • Ideal For: Advanced athletes, those with significant training experience, or athletes with more available time
