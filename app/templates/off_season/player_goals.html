SMART Goals Framework

Setting SMART goals is a proven way to improve your performance during the off-season:

  • Specific - Clear, concrete objectives
  • Measurable - Quantifiable metrics for tracking progress
  • Achievable - Challenging but realistic
  • Relevant - Aligned with ultimate frisbee performance
  • Time-bound - Set within the phase timeframe

Set 2-3 goals for each training phase to focus your efforts and track your improvement.

Example SMART Goals
  • Increase vertical jump by 2 inches by the end of the Foundation Phase
  • Complete 3 strength workouts per week during the Strength Development Phase
  • Improve forehand huck accuracy from 60% to 75% by the end of the Integration Phase
