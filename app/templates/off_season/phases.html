Phase Transition Tips
Moving Between Phases
  • Complete a performance assessment at the end of each phase to track progress
  • Take 2-3 days of active recovery between phases
  • Review your goals and adjust as needed for the upcoming phase
  • Prepare equipment and schedule for the new phase's requirements
Adapting the Program
  • If you're behind schedule, don't skip ahead—continue with the current phase
  • If you're excelling, focus on quality over quantity rather than rushing ahead
  • Adjust workout intensity based on your recovery capacity
  • Maintain consistent communication with your coach about your progress
