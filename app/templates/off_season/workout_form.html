    # Lower Body Strength Workout

## Warm-up (10 minutes)
- 5 minutes light cardio (jogging, jumping jacks, etc.)
- Dynamic stretching
- Activation exercises (glute bridges, bodyweight squats)

## Main Workout

### Strength Circuit (3-4 rounds)
1. **Squats** - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
   - Focus on proper form with knees tracking over toes
   - Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

2. **Romanian Deadlifts** - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
   - Focus on hip hinge movement and flat back
   - Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

3. **Walking Lunges** - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
   - Take long strides and keep torso upright
   - Rest 60 seconds between sets

4. **Calf Raises** - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
   - Full range of motion, pause at top
   - Rest 45 seconds between sets

### Core Finisher
- Plank: 3 sets of 30-45 seconds
- Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
- Supermans: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

## Cool Down (5 minutes)
- Static stretching for all major muscle groups
- Focus on hamstrings, quads, calves, and lower back

## Notes
- Adjust weights based on your current strength level
- Proper form is more important than heavy weight
- If you don't have access to weights, use bodyweight variations
                        

    # Throwing Technique Workout

## Warm-up (10 minutes)
- Light jog (5 minutes)
- Dynamic stretching focusing on shoulders, arms, and core
- Wrist mobility exercises

## Throwing Progression

### Basic Technique (15 minutes)
1. **Grip Practice** - 5 minutes
   - Backhand: practice proper grip and wrist snap
   - Forehand: practice proper grip and wrist flick

2. **Short Throws** - 10 minutes
   - Partner up at 5-10 yards distance
   - Focus on clean release and spin
   - 50 backhands, 50 forehands

### Intermediate Throws (20 minutes)
1. **Medium Distance** - 10 minutes
   - Increase to 15-20 yards
   - Focus on maintaining proper form with increased power
   - 30 backhands, 30 forehands

2. **Throwing on the Move** - 10 minutes
   - Practice throwing while moving laterally
   - Practice pivoting and throwing
   - 20 reps each direction

### Advanced Techniques (15 minutes)
Choose 2-3 of these to focus on:
- Inside-out throws
- Outside-in throws
- Hammers
- Scoobers
- High-release backhands

## Accuracy Drills (15 minutes)
1. **Target Practice**
   - Set up targets or use cones
   - Practice hitting specific spots from various distances
   - 10 attempts per target

2. **Throwing Windows**
   - Have a partner create "windows" with arms
   - Practice throwing through the windows
   - Vary height and width of windows

## Cool Down (5 minutes)
- Light stretching
- Wrist and shoulder recovery exercises

## Notes
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Record yourself to analyze form if possible
- If working alone, use targets or throw against a wall
                        

    # Ultimate-Specific Conditioning Workout

## Warm-up (10 minutes)
- Light jog (3 minutes)
- Dynamic stretching (3 minutes)
- Movement preparation (4 minutes):
  - High knees
  - Butt kicks
  - Lateral shuffles
  - Carioca
  - Acceleration/deceleration practice

## Main Workout

### Interval Training (20 minutes)
**Shuttle Sprints**
- Set up cones at 5, 10, and 20 yards
- Sprint to each cone and back to start
- Rest 30 seconds between sets
- Complete 6-8 sets

**Change of Direction Drills**
- Set up a zigzag pattern with cones
- Sprint through pattern focusing on sharp cuts
- Rest 45 seconds between sets
- Complete 6 sets

### Ultimate-Specific Conditioning (15 minutes)
**Cutting Patterns**
- Practice various cutting patterns at full speed:
  - In-cuts
  - Out-cuts
  - Deep cuts
  - Clearing cuts
- 30 seconds work, 30 seconds rest
- 10 total rounds

### Endurance Finisher (10 minutes)
**"The 120s"**
- 20 seconds sprint
- 10 seconds rest
- 20 seconds sprint
- 10 seconds rest
- 20 seconds sprint
- 10 seconds rest
- 20 seconds sprint
- 10 seconds rest
- Repeat for 2 total rounds with 1 minute rest between rounds

## Cool Down (5 minutes)
- Light jogging (2 minutes)
- Static stretching (3 minutes)

## Notes
- Adjust work/rest ratios based on your fitness level
- Focus on quality of movement and proper cutting technique
- Track your recovery between intervals to monitor progress
