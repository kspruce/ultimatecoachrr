Workout Types Guide
Strength

Focuses on building raw strength and muscle development. These workouts typically involve resistance training with weights, bands, or bodyweight exercises.

Speed

Designed to improve acceleration, top speed, and running mechanics. These workouts include sprint drills, technique work, and high-velocity movements.

Power

Combines strength and speed to develop explosive movements. These workouts focus on plyometrics, Olympic lifts, and other explosive exercises.

Endurance

Builds cardiovascular capacity and stamina. These workouts include interval training, tempo runs, and other sustained effort activities.

Agility

Improves change of direction, footwork, and reaction time. These workouts include ladder drills, cone patterns, and reactive movements.

Throwing

Focuses on disc skills, throwing mechanics, and accuracy. These workouts include various throwing drills, progressions, and technique work.
