Cutting Skills Definitions
Open Deep
A cut to the open side of the field, moving away from the handler.
Open Under
A cut to the open side of the field, moving toward the handler.
Break Deep
A cut to the break side of the field, moving away from the handler.
Break Under
A cut to the break side of the field, moving toward the handler.
Open Looked Off
Player was open but the throw wasn't made.
Guarded Looked Off
Player was covered by a defender and the throw wasn't made.
Open Thrown To
Player was open and received the throw.
Guarded Thrown To
Player was covered but still received the throw.
Success Rate
Percentage of cuts that resulted in the player receiving the disc (Open Thrown To + Guarded Thrown To) / Total Attempts.
