Radar Chart Alias Information

Some metrics on the defensive radar chart are aliases for D-Line Conversion Rate to provide a more intuitive chart. This is because a player's direct contribution to a "Break" or "Defensive Efficiency" is measured by how often their D-line scores when they are on the field.

  • Break Percentage (Player): This is set to the player's D-Line Conversion Rate.
  • Defensive Efficiency (Player): This is also set to the player's D-Line Conversion Rate.

The team's values for these metrics are calculated independently based on overall team performance.
