Notes on Calculations
Team Averages vs. Team Totals

Team metrics are calculated as averages across all players, not as team totals divided by total points. This is why the team average shows values like 5.0 games played, 12.5 points played, etc.

Conversion Rates

Conversion rates are calculated as "points scored containing the player / points played by the player" for both O-line and D-line.

Defensive Metrics Aliases

For individual players, both "Break Percentage" and "Defensive Efficiency" are aliases for the player's D-Line Conversion Rate. This is because a player's direct contribution to a "Break" or "Defensive Efficiency" is measured by how often their D-line scores when they are on the field.
