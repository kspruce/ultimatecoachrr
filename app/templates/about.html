About Ultimate Coach

Ultimate Coach is a comprehensive web application designed to help Ultimate Frisbee coaches manage their teams, track game statistics, analyze performance, and plan training sessions.

Features

  • Team Management: Manage your roster, assign roles, and track player information.
  • Game Management: Track game results, record detailed statistics, and analyze performance.
  • Statistics: View comprehensive statistics and visualizations of team and player performance.
  • Clip Library: Store and organize video clips of games and plays.
  • Scouting Reports: Create and manage scouting reports for opposing teams.
  • Session Plans: Create and manage practice plans and drills.
