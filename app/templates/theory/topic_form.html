How to Use Imgur for Images
  1. Go to Imgur.com/upload
  2. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Choose Photo/Video"
  3. Once uploaded, right-click on the image and select "Copy image address"
  4. Paste the copied URL into the Image URL field above
  5. The URL should look like: https://i.imgur.com/abcXYZ.jpg
Note: You don't need an Imgur account to upload images, but creating one allows you to manage your uploads.
